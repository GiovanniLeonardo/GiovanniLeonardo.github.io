About Me

## Welcome to my page.

I’m a seasoned IT leader and Network Product Manager based in the North Metro Atlanta area. My journey in technology began in NJ and NY, over the past 25+ years, I’ve had the privilege of driving enterprise network and digital infrastructure strategies for industry leaders like **Cortel Business Systems, AT&T, GE, and currently, GE Vernova.

My career bridges the gap between deep technical execution and high-level business strategy. I’ve built my expertise from the ground up—starting with hands-on IT support, software, and hardware, and evolving into designing resilient global data and voice networks, leading pre-sales engineering, and managing enterprise product lifecycles.

Today, my focus centers on the intersection of network product management, technology automation, and financial management. I specialize in optimizing global telecom procurement, managing complex IT lifecycles, and negotiating with worldwide suppliers to build efficient, scalable enterprise networks.

When I’m not streamlining global infrastructure or building custom apps and web tools, you can usually find me exploring the outdoors, tackling a DIY project, or planning my next backpacking adventure.
